Wedding Details

Charles River Museum of Industry and Innovation
154 Moody St.
Waltham, MA 02453

The Charles River Museum of Industry and Innovation is listed on the National Register of Historic Places as America's first factory. It has a museum on the first floor dedicated to feats of engineering and a beautiful industrial event space on the second floor.

We will have the ceremony and reception upstairs, with cocktail hour in between in the museum below!

If arriving by car:

Park in the Embassy Street Lot located at 11 Cooper Street. From there you will follow signs to the museum over a footbridge and down a walkway to the front door of the museum.

If arriving by commuter rail:

Take the Fitchburg line from North Station and get off at Waltham stop. Walk through Landry Park and you will find the museum at the end of the walkway.
